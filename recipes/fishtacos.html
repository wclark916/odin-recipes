Fish Tacos

Simple fish taco recipe that is a clone of one of my favorite dishes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup mexican beer

    • I prefer corona

  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 2 lbs rockfish

    • You can use cod or halibut but I like the flavor added by rockfish.

  • Rubio's white sauce clone
    • <--link to white sauce recipe-->
  • 1 package corn tortillas
  • Salsa of choice
  • Oil for frying
  • 2 limes

I make the white sauce in advance and chill in fridgerator. Also, salt to taste. You can add garlic and onion powder to batter for more flavor.

Steps

  1. Step 1: Make white sauce
  2. Step 2: Mix flour and beer into a bowl. 1 cup leaves a bit of a 12oz remaining if you're thirsty....
  3. Step 3: Shred cabbage if not done so and set aside.
  4. Step 4: Heat oil for frying
  5. Step 5: Cut or portion fish into pieces, I prefer 1" by 3". Its a matter of preference. I feel its a little easier to manage this way.
  6. Step 6: Dip your fish portions into batter one at a time making sure they are well coated. drop them into the fryer and cook until done. Removing them and allowing to cool slightly.
  7. Step 7: Assemble Tacos, I start with a tortilla, add white sauce and then fish followed by cabbage. Then add salsa and a sqeeze of lime juice from a wedge.
  8. Step 8: Enjoy
