Scrambled Eggs

This has become a go to way of preparing this breakfast dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1.5 Tbls butter
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1: crack eggs into a mixing bowl and mix until combined
  2. Step 2: Add salt and stirr into eggs
  3. Step 3: Heat pan under medium heat.
  4. Step 4: Add eggs stirring occasionally to avoid sticking onto bottom of pan
  5. Step 5: Remove eggs once cooked(may look runny) and enjoy
